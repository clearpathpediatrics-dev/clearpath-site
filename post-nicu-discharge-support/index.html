Common questions

What is post-NICU care navigation?

Post-NICU care navigation is non-medical support that helps families organize the follow-up care a NICU graduate needs — appointments, medications, referrals, and paperwork. ClearPath's RNs help you understand and coordinate what your child's providers have already prescribed; we do not provide medical care or treatment.

Do you replace my baby's doctors?

No. ClearPath complements your child's licensed medical team. We handle the organization, preparation, and coordination between visits so you can focus on your baby — all clinical decisions stay with your providers.

Important: ClearPath Pediatrics does not provide medical care, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services. All medical decisions remain with your child's licensed healthcare providers. In an emergency, call 911.
