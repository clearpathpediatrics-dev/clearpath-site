Common questions

Do you serve families outside Phoenix?

Yes. ClearPath Pediatrics is based in Phoenix, Arizona, and delivers services virtually, so we can support families across the Valley and nationwide. Reach out to confirm availability in your area.

Is ClearPath a doctor's office?

No. ClearPath is a private, fee-for-service care navigation and education service — not a medical practice. We help you organize, understand, and prepare for the care your licensed providers direct. All medical decisions stay with your child's healthcare team.

Important: ClearPath Pediatrics does not provide medical care, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services. All medical decisions remain with your child's licensed healthcare providers. In an emergency, call 911.
